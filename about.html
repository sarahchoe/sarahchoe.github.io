About

In 2000, Dr. Dan Smith, Dr. Mark Sheehan, and Reverend Setan Lee founded Christian Medical Ministry to Cambodia/Jeremiah's Hope or CMMCJH. Their vision: offer excellent medical care to the poor and quaility medical education to the healthcare community of Cambodia.

We are a 501c charitable organization dedicated to serving the poor population of Cambodia.
